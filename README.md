DeepRead

DeepRead is an AI-powered tool that lets you chat with your PDFs. Ask questions in natural language and get accurate, context-aware answers directly from your documents using RAG with Gemini 2.5 API.

Unlike generic AI responses, DeepRead reads your files first, ensuring answers are grounded in your content.
